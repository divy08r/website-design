Metrics- Track And Improve Your Performance

As a seller, making sure that your products are dispatched on time is an absolute necessity. However, while processing orders, many sellers often breach the RTD (Ready to Dispatch) period. RTD, in simple words, is the total time period within which a seller has to get his products prepared for dispatch. This doesn’t just affect the seller’s performance ratings but it also might lead to the customers cancelling the order.

Tips to improve your seller performance

Returns: Frequent returns can lead to the delisting of your account.
Seller cancellation: The seller cancellation rate should not exceed 0.5%. If you cross this threshold, you are liable to be charged an additional commission fee depending on whether you cancelled before/after marking RTD.
Pick up reattempts: This rate shouldn't exceed 3%. May affect your performance.

Seller Tiering Overview

When you sell on Flipkart, the better you perform, the more benefits you can enjoy. Your performance is evaluated and rewarded on the basis of this you are categorized as a Bronze seller, Silver seller or Gold seller. This rating is valid for a period of 90 days and once you achieve all the criteria for the next tier, it will be upgraded.

Why do products get returned

As a seller you should work towards increasing your sales but you should also focus on how you can decrease returns. In order to do this, it is important for you to understand the reasons why products are returned. Product returns are of two types:
Customer returns: Product isn’t matching with the product description
Courier returns: Customer was not reachable in all 3 attempts
